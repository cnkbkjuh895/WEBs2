The Winter Nights in your Memories: I imagine a man sipping cocoa, and staring out a wall-wide window. The light inside illuminates out, and just out of it's radius are two winter-clad children playing, frozen still in a time long ago. Further, I have memories of posting this song, with this same image in my head, onto a Discord server with my friends. Though I haven't, and probably never will.
